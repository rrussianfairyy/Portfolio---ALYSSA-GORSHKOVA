PROJECT 01

Milford Sound - Brand Identity





Brief:

The task is to create a visual brand identity for a New Zealand subject and develop a style
guide that will support the visual design of an infographic.

I chose Milford Sound, a stunning fjord on New Zealand's South Island. My research highlighted
Milford Sound as one of New Zealand's most cherished natural wonders, steeped in history and
remarkable beauty. To convey this richness and appeal, I selected a color palette inspired by
Milford Sound’s landscape to create a design that reflects its unique atmosphere.

The style guide provides detailed guidelines for using logos, iconography, color schemes, and
typography throughout the infographic.

PROJECT 02

Solis - Candle Package Design

Design Challenge:

Design premium packaging for Solis, an organic, high-end scented candle brand. The packaging
should reflect the brand’s ethos of mindfulness, minimalism, and comfort, while visually
capturing the essence of natural landscapes and phenomena that inspire each candle. The design
must evoke a sense of luxury and storytelling, inviting the user into an immersive sensory experience.

PROJECT 03

Coca Cola - Horror Advertisement

Concept:
